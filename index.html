Source: Beginner's Guide to Peptide Therapy (Jan 2026)
What are peptides?
Peptides are strings of amino acids — the building blocks of proteins. The body produces them naturally (endogenous), but they can also be administered externally (exogenous) to support specific biological functions.
20 amino acidsThousands of sequencesDiverse applications
Routes of Administration
MethodSubcutaneous injection (most common)
Why injectable?Peptides degrade in the gut. Oral bioavailability as low as 1–2%
StorageFridge at ~4°C / 39°F. Up to 28 days once reconstituted
Never shakeSwirl gently only — shaking degrades the peptide
Peptide types by goal
Weight Loss — GLP-1 RAs
Tirzepatide, Semaglutide
▾
How they work
Slow digestion, increase satiety, reduce appetite, and create milder glucose spikes — all reducing caloric intake.
Tirzepatide vs Semaglutide
Both highly effective. Tirzepatide subjects lost 4–12 more lbs in a 2021 study. Semaglutide more widely accessible.
✕ GLP-1s must always be injected alone — never mix.
Body Recomposition
GH Secretagogues — CJC, Ipamorelin, Tesamorelin
▾
How they work
Stimulate the pituitary to release GH, which promotes muscle growth and regulates body fat via IGF-1.
Ipamorelin
Selective GHRP — minimal cortisol/prolactin elevation. Cleanest GH secretagogue. Best before bed on empty stomach.
Tesamorelin
FDA-approved for HIV lipodystrophy. Used off-label for abdominal fat loss and lean mass. Stack with Ipamorelin.
Your active: Tesamorelin, Ipamorelin, TB-500, BPC-157/TB blend, KLOW blend.
Mitochondrial & Longevity
SS-31, MOTS-C, NAD+
▾
SS-31
Targets the inner mitochondrial membrane. Reduces oxidative stress, supports cardiac health. Your current active protocol.
MOTS-C
Mimics exercise at cellular level. Improves insulin sensitivity. You ran 4 weeks — stopped due to ISR. Try smaller vials, more dilute solution if retrying.
Mixing rule
SS-31, MOTS-C, NAD+ can be mixed with each other but not with other peptide categories.
Safety
Immunogenicity
Synthetic peptides can trigger immune responses. ISR (injection site reactions) are the most common mild form.
GH Secretagogues + Cancer
Tesamorelin, CJC-1295, Ipamorelin are contraindicated for anyone with active cancer or cancer history.
Legal status (US)
Governed by Section 503A of the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act. Many peptides are in a legal gray area — not prohibited, but not on the FDA's 503A bulks list.
